Establishment : Considering the student as the focal point, the institute was founded at the mouth of Gudi Padwa in 1919 by seven school students.
Approach : The institution has adopted the broad idea to provide educational facilities to the needy students and to promote their overall development.
Goals : Generating intelligent, rich and cultured citizens. To create a strong, cordial and purely patriotic society which is intended for Swami Vivekananda. Working separately from caste, religion, and politics.
Scope : The organization operates mainly in the areas of Chinchpokali, Satarasta, Bhayakhala, Lalbagh and Nam Joshi, known as Girgaon in central Mumbai.
Membership : Any student studying in Class VIII and above can become a member of the institution. The only organization offering a lifetime membership for just one rupee. Students of any caste religion are allowed to become members.
Member Family : Today there are more than 16000 members of the organization. Many of them are graduates, highly educated and are working in the fields of doctors, professors, lawyers, engineers, journalists, art or sports.
